Mission

Our goal is to bring mental health education and treatment to underprivileged communities. To aid people who are misdiagnosed, untreated and stigmatized.

Impact

We aim to place afflicted people in a more comfortable situation in all aspects of their life: education, treatment, medicine, housing, saftey from addiction, and safety from abuse.
